How to Apply for REAP 2026?

The application process is entirely online. Candidates must follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official REAP 2026 website: reap2026.in
  2. Navigate to the "Application Form" or "Register for REAP 2026" link.
  3. Complete the initial registration by providing basic details.
  4. Fill out the detailed application form with academic and personal information.
  5. Upload scanned copies of your photograph and signature as per specifications.
  6. Pay the required application fee through the online payment gateway.
  7. Submit the form and save a copy of the confirmation page for future reference.

REAP 2026 Application Fee & Payment

To complete the registration, candidates must pay a non-refundable application and counselling fee. Based on previous years, the fee is expected to be ₹590.

The fee must be paid online through one of the following methods:

  • Credit Card
  • Debit Card
  • Net Banking
  • UPI Payments

Note: The fee is non-refundable under any circumstances. Ensure all details are correct before proceeding with the payment.
